The Mechanism

In short:

1. Activation: The machine activates upon user approach.
2. Embrasure Opening: Users insert waste through a small opening leading to a segregation plate.
3. Primary Segregation: Sensors detect plastic content (>60%) and divert it to a separate compartment.
4. Crushing Compartment: Waste with less than 60% plastic falls onto a crusher for size reduction.
5. Vibration Plate: Small vibrations in the biocompartment further segregate particles.
6. Composting Unit: Waste particles reach the composting unit with BSF larvae.
7. Optimal Conditions: Larvae decompose organic waste in optimal temperature, humidity, and moisture conditions.
8. Natural Processing: Organic waste breaks down naturally within 20 days using BSF larvae, no chemicals involved.
