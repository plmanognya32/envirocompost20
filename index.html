But,
what is
Envirocompost20?

The "Envirocompost 20" offers an innovative and a comprehensive solution by accepting both plastic and biological waste from users. The machine separates biodegradable and non-biodegradable waste, processing them differently for maximum efficiency.

It comes as a much-needed solution to the pressing issue of waste disposal faced globally. With landfills running out of space due to the increasing waste production, there is an urgent need for an efficient and sustainable waste management system.
