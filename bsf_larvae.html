The Black Soldier Fly Larvae

Black soldier fly larvae, have the very useful capacity of being able to rapidly degrade organic biowaste that is produced by markets, food industries and restaurants. They are a valuable source of protein, vitamins, fats, amino acids and essential nutrients, including calcium, iron, magnesium, sodium, zinc, and potassium. They also have a low carbon footprint and are not disease vectors.

Pet Feed

They are rich in protein, essential amino acids, and beneficial fats, making them a valuable dietary supplement. They are easily digestible and safe for pets when farmed under controlled conditions.

Organically Rich Compost

Organically rich compost made by Black Soldier Fly (BSF) larvae offers nutrient-rich soil, improves soil structure, suppresses plant diseases, reduces carbon footprint, supports sustainability, and enhances crop yield and quality.

Fats, Oils and Biodiesel from BSFL

Biodiesel produced from Black Soldier Fly (BSF) larvae offers a sustainable and renewable alternative to traditional diesel fuel. It reduces greenhouse gas emissions, utilizes organic waste as feedstock, and is biodegradable, promoting environmental sustainability and waste reduction.
